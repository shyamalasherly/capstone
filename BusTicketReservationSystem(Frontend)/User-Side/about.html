About Us

Nurtured from the seed of a single idea ‘to empower the traveller with easy & instant travel bookings providing comprehensive choices’, BookBuses is a pioneer in India’s online travel space. Founded by Deep Kalra in the year 2000, BookBuses began its journey by serving the US-India travel market with best-value products & services, powered by robust
technology and round-the-clock customer support. redBus has a global presence with operations across Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Colombia and Peru apart from India. After successfully consolidating its position as a customer-first brand, known for its reliability and transparency, BookBuses launched its India operations. As low-cost flight carriers were introduced in India, the number of Indians opting for online travel solutions also increased rapidly. And BookBusess answered the call of the hour, by bringing the convenience of booking flights, hotels, and holiday packages in just a few clicks.
